## KEY FINDINGS

### Strengths:
1. **Strong International Presence**: Athletics (Christine Mboma), Cricket (T20 World Cup), Rugby (7 consecutive World Cups)
2. **Recent Infrastructure**: Cricket Namibia's first official stadium (2025)
3. **Growing Women's Sports**: Women's rugby, netball, football all experiencing growth
4. **Youth Development**: Multiple federations have active youth programs
5. **Regional Success**: Multiple medals at AUSC Region 5 Youth Games

### Challenges:
1. **Funding**: Many federations operate on limited budgets (NAWISA on zero budget)
2. **Facilities**: Lack of proper training facilities (athletes training barefoot)
3. **Inactive Federations**: Some federations appear dormant (Softball, Baseball, Lacrosse, Orienteering)
4. **Leadership Gaps**: Some federations have vacant Secretary General positions

### Recent Milestones (2024-2025):
- AFCON knockout stage (Football - first time)
- Paris 2024 Olympics participation (4 athletes)
- Paris 2024 Paralympics medals (Ananias Shikongo - silver & bronze)
- First official cricket stadium opening
- 4th consecutive T20 World Cup qualification (Cricket)
- Historic bronze medal in inline hockey at World Games 2025
- Multiple Region 5 Championships victories

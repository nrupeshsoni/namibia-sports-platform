## Notes

1. **Weightlifting vs Powerlifting:** PWFN covers both in Namibia; NNOC lists them as Namibian Powerlifting & Weightlifting Federation.
2. **NNSSU vs NSSU vs NNSU:** NNSU (National Sport Student Union) is the Ministry-linked students body; NSSU (Namibia Schools Sport Union) is for school learners. No distinct “NNSSU” found.
3. **TISAN vs NTSGF:** TISAN = Tertiary Institutes (university sport); NTSGF = Namibia Traditional Sport and Games Federation (traditional/indigenous sports). Both are included.
4. **NUFS:** NUFSA has no public website or social media; contact mainly via kevinlubinda@gmail.com or Ministry of Defence.
